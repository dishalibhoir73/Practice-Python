Company Name: NovaTech Solutions

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 18 paid leaves per year, including casual and sick leave.
Unused leaves cannot be carried forward to the next year.
Prior approval from the reporting manager is required for planned leaves.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
WFH is subject to performance and role requirements.
Employees must be available during core working hours.

Working Hours Policy:
Standard working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are expected to log at least 8 hours of productive work per day.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain professional behavior at the workplace.
Harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior will result in disciplinary action.
Company assets must be used responsibly.

Attendance Policy:
Employees must mark daily attendance using the company portal.
Repeated late arrivals may impact performance evaluations.

Termination Policy:
Either party must provide a 30-day notice period before termination.
Immediate termination may occur in case of policy violations.

Data Security Policy:
Employees must not share confidential company data with external parties.
Use of unauthorized storage devices is strictly prohibited.
